# ALPAS Time and Attendance System — Help

This Help guide is tailored to your role and loads automatically when you click the Help button.

## Available Guides

- **Employee Guide** — Clock In/Out, Attendance, Leave, Overtime, Corrections, Profile
- **Department Head Guide** — Team Dashboard, Approvals (Leave, OT, Corrections, Conversions)
- **HR Guide** — Employee Management, Attendance Monitoring, Leave Adjustments, Approvals, Reports
- **Administrator Guide** — Departments, Audit Logs, System Settings, and all HR features

## Quick Tips

- All times in the system are in **Philippine Standard Time (PHT, UTC+8)**.
- You can clock out before **6:00 AM** the next morning for overnight shifts.
- Overtime must be filed within **15 days** of the shift or it expires permanently.
- Emergency Leave and Sick Leave share a combined balance of **15 days per year**.
- Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, and a number.
