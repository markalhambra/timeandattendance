## 1. Logging In

**Purpose:** Access the system using your company credentials.

**Steps:**
1. Open the app in your browser.
2. Enter your company **email address**.
3. Enter your **password**. Click the eye icon to show or hide it.
4. Click **Sign In**.
5. You will be taken to your **Dashboard** automatically.

> **Tip:** If you see a spinner, the system is loading — wait a moment before trying again.

> **Common Error:** "Invalid credentials" — double-check your email spelling and make sure Caps Lock is off.

## 2. Forgot / Reset Password

**Purpose:** Recover access if you forgot your password.

**Steps:**
1. On the Login page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your company email address and click **Send Reset Link**.
3. Check your email inbox (also check Spam). The link is valid for **1 hour**.
4. Click the link, enter your new password twice, and click **Reset Password**.
5. You will be returned to the Login page.

> **Password requirements:** At least 8 characters, must include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one number.

## 4. Clock In and Clock Out

**Purpose:** Record your daily work hours.

### How to Clock In
1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click **Clock In** in the Clock widget.
3. Allow your browser to access your location if prompted.
4. If you are **within 200 meters of the office**, your status is set to **On-Site** automatically.
5. If you are **outside the office**, a menu appears — choose your work type:
   - **WFH** — Working from Home
   - **OB** — Official Business (off-site work)
   - **On-Site** — if you are on-site but GPS is inaccurate
6. Click **Confirm**. Your clock-in time is saved.

> **Tip:** Clock in the moment you start work — the system records the exact time you click.

### How to Clock Out
1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click **Clock Out** in the Clock widget.
3. Allow location access if prompted.
4. Your total hours and any overtime are calculated and saved automatically.

> **Overnight Shifts:** If you work past midnight and finish before **6:00 AM** the next morning, you can still clock out using the app. The system automatically matches your clock-out to your previous day's clock-in.

> **Common Error — "No clock-in found for today":** If you forgot to clock in, or if it is already past 6:00 AM the next day, use the Attendance Correction feature (Section 6).

## 6. Requesting an Attendance Correction

**Purpose:** Fix an incorrect or missing clock-in or clock-out on a past working day.

**Navigation:** Sidebar → **Attendance** → click **Correct** on the record

> **Important:** Corrections can only be filed for **past dates**. You cannot correct today or future dates. Wait until the next working day.

**Steps:**
1. Go to **Attendance** and find the record that needs fixing.
2. Click the **Correct** link on that row.
3. The correction form appears with three fields:
   - **Corrected Clock In** — enter the correct clock-in time.
   - **Corrected Clock Out** — enter the correct clock-out time.
   - **Reason** — explain why the correction is needed (required).
4. **For overnight shifts** (you clocked out after midnight, on the next calendar day):
   - Tick the checkbox labelled **"Clock-out is on the next day (overnight)"**.
   - The Clock Out picker will automatically switch to the next calendar day.
   - Enter the correct time (for example, 03:00 AM).
5. Click **Submit Request**.
6. Your request is sent to your Department Head for review. You will be notified when it is approved or rejected.

> **Tip:** Write a clear, specific reason. Vague reasons are more likely to be rejected.

> **Why does my Hours column show a negative number?** This happens when the clock-out time was saved before the clock-in time — common in overnight shift corrections that did not use the overnight checkbox. File a new correction and use the checkbox to fix it.

## 7. Filing a Leave Request

**Purpose:** Apply for approved time off using your leave credits.

**Navigation:** Sidebar → **Leave** → click **File Leave**

**Steps:**
1. Click **Leave** in the sidebar.
2. Click the **File Leave** button at the top of the page.
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Leave Type** — choose from the list below.
   - **Leave Duration** — Full Day, Half Day Morning, or Half Day Afternoon.
   - **Start Date** — first day of leave.
   - **End Date** — last day of leave.
   - **Reason** — brief explanation.
4. Click **File Leave**.
5. Your request goes to your Department Head for approval. You will be notified of the outcome.

> **Half-day leaves** are only available for Vacation Leave, Sick Leave, and Emergency Leave. The start and end date must be the same day for half-day requests.

**Leave Types:**

| Leave Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Vacation Leave (VL) | Personal time off |
| Sick Leave (SL) | Illness or medical appointments |
| Emergency Leave | Urgent personal or family situations. Shares the 15-day Sick Leave balance. |
| Pamilya Muna Leave (PML) | Family-related leave |
| Solo Parent Leave | For employees with a valid Solo Parent ID |
| Magna Carta Leave | For qualified medical procedures under RA 9710 |
| Maternity Leave | For maternity purposes |
| Paternity Leave | For fathers upon the birth of a child |
| Bereavement Leave | Death of an immediate family member |
| Special Medical Leave (SML) | Prolonged illness |
| Leave Without Pay (LWOP) | Unpaid leave — no balance required, always available |

> **Tenure Requirement:** Most leave types require at least **6 months of employment**. If you are under 6 months, only **LWOP** is available. A "File LWOP" button appears on the Leave page.

> **Common Error — "Insufficient balance":** Emergency Leave uses your Sick Leave balance. They share a combined pool of 15 days. Check your available balance before filing.

## 9. Overtime — Filing and Converting

**Purpose:** Get credit for working more than 9 hours in a day.

**Navigation:** Sidebar → **Overtime**

### How Overtime Is Recorded
When you clock out after more than **9 hours**, the extra time is automatically saved as a **Draft**. Drafts are private — only you can see them. You must **file** a draft within **15 days** or it expires permanently.

### Step 1: File Your Overtime Request
1. Click **Overtime** in the sidebar.
2. In the Overtime Records table, find records marked **DRAFT**.
3. Click **File OT** on the record.
4. Enter a **Reason** for the overtime.
5. Click **Submit** — your request is sent to your Department Head for approval.

> **Warning:** Do not wait. Drafts expire after 15 days and cannot be recovered.

### Step 2: Convert Approved Overtime to Leave Credits
Once your overtime is **Approved**, you can convert it:
- **CTO** (Compensatory Time Off) — minimum **4 hours** required
- **CDO** (Credit Day Off) — minimum **8 hours** required

**Steps:**
1. Click **Convert OT** at the top of the Overtime page.
2. Choose the conversion type: **CTO** or **CDO**.
3. Pick a **Scheduled Date** — the day you plan to use it.
4. Check the boxes next to the approved overtime records to include.
5. Enter hours to convert, or leave blank to convert all.
6. Click **Convert** — sent to your Department Head for approval.

> **Tip:** Approved OT credits expire **6 months** after approval. Convert before the expiry date shown in the table.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| DRAFT | Saved but not yet filed |
| AWAITING HR | Filed, waiting for approval |
| APPROVED | Approved and ready to convert |
| REJECTED | Not approved |
| EXPIRED | Filing or credit window has passed |

## 14. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I forgot to clock in. What do I do?**
A: Go to Attendance, find the date, click Correct, and submit a correction with a clear reason.

**Q: My hours show as a negative number (e.g. -5.4h). Why?**
A: The clock-out time was saved before the clock-in time — this commonly happens with overnight shift corrections that did not use the overnight checkbox. File a new correction and tick "Clock-out is on the next day (overnight)".

**Q: The "File Leave" button is not visible.**
A: You may have less than 6 months of employment. Use the "File LWOP" button instead. The Leave page shows when you become eligible.

**Q: My overtime draft disappeared.**
A: Drafts expire after 15 days if not filed. File as soon as possible after working overtime.

**Q: Can I cancel an approved leave?**
A: No — only Pending leaves can be cancelled from the app. Contact HR for approved leaves.

**Q: What time zone does the system use?**
A: Philippine Standard Time (PHT, UTC+8). All times are in Philippine time.

**Q: Who approves my requests?**
A: Your Department Head is the first reviewer. HR may perform a final review after that.
